Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Neverday is a small creative studio based in Kansas City. We collect as little as we can, we never sell your information, and we only ask for what we need to talk to you about a project. This page explains exactly what that means.

01 Who we are

In this policy, "Neverday," "we," "us," and "our" refer to Neverday, a creative studio operating out of Kansas City. This policy applies to this website and any project inquiries you send us through it. If you have questions about anything here, email us at hello@neverday.com.

02 Information we collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to give us. The main way that happens is the "Start a project" form on our home page. When you fill it out, you may share:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your business or brand name
  • What you're looking for — the services, budget, and timeline you select
  • The project details you write, and how you heard about us

The form works by opening your own email app with these details pre-filled — nothing is submitted to a database on this site. The information reaches us only when you press send in your email client. You can also reach us directly at hello@neverday.com.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our host may automatically log basic technical information when you visit — such as your IP address, browser type, device, and the pages you view. This is standard server logging used to keep the site secure and running. We do not use advertising trackers, and the site does not set marketing or analytics cookies.

03 How we use your information

We use the information you send us to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and discuss a potential project
  • Prepare proposals, estimates, and scopes of work
  • Carry out and communicate about work you hire us to do
  • Keep records of our correspondence and agreements

We will not use your contact details to send you marketing you didn't ask for, and we will never sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

04 How your information is shared

We keep your information to ourselves. We only share it in a few limited situations:

  • Service providers we rely on to operate — for example, our website host and our email provider — who process information on our behalf.
  • Legal reasons, if we're required to by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • A business transfer, such as if the studio is ever merged with or acquired by another company.

05 Hosting and third parties

This site is hosted on Vercel, which processes requests and may keep standard server logs on our behalf. Communication you send through the form travels through your own email provider and is received by our email provider. These companies handle data under their own privacy terms. We use self-hosted animation libraries on this site and do not embed third-party advertising or social-media tracking scripts.

06 Cookies

This website does not use cookies for advertising or analytics. Our host may use strictly necessary cookies to deliver and secure the site. If we add analytics or other cookies in the future, we'll update this policy first.

07 How long we keep it

We keep project inquiries and correspondence for as long as we need them to respond to you, carry out work, and meet our legal and accounting obligations. When information is no longer needed, we delete it or keep it only in archived form. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

08 Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to ask us to stop using it. To make any of these requests, just email hello@neverday.com and we'll take care of it. We won't charge you or treat you differently for exercising these rights.

09 Children's privacy

This site and our services are meant for businesses and adults. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has sent us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

10 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as our studio and the site evolve. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected here, so it's worth checking back occasionally.

11 Contact us

Questions, requests, or anything about your privacy? Email us at hello@neverday.com — a real person will get back to you.
