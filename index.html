Process

Four steps, no mystery. You'll always know exactly where we are — even the unglamorous parts. (hover a step for the honest version.)

01

Get to know you

We start with a long conversation — the story, the regulars, the thing you'd never put on the menu but everyone asks for.

Between us — we turn up with too many questions and leave with three pages of notes and a new favourite dish.

02

Find the angle

We come back with one sharp direction, argued for — not ten safe ones to average out into mush.

Between us — we fight about it for a week first, so you never have to sit through the bad ideas.

03

Make it real

Design and build happen side by side, so what you sign off is what actually ships — in the browser, not a flat picture of one.

Between us — no "that's not what the mockup looked like." The mockup was the website.

04

Send it off

We launch, hand over the keys, and stay close through the first busy stretch while everything settles.

Between us — you will not get a goodbye Loom and a cheerful "good luck out there!"
